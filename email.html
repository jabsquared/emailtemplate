My team and I are doing market research on new application that we're actively building called Uphealth.

Uphealth is simple predictive health analytics. How does it work? The application simply shows a list of symptoms of possible issues with your health. Each time the user experiences one of the symptoms listed the user would just tap on the symptom. We log the date and amount of times the user taps on the symptom, and cross reference that data to a number of other possible illness's correlated with that symptom. Our technology will then begin to build a profile on the user, and then customize their feed of possible symptoms in effort of narrowing down what illness's the user is most prone to having.
